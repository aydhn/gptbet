# Corridor Registry Federation and Attestation Hubs Architecture

The Federation Ecosystem layer builds upon the corridor registries and continuity attestation exchanges established in previous phases. It introduces a `federated registry` structure, allowing nodes to link and share currentness without blindly assuming runtime authority.

Key concepts:
- **Federation Preserves Lineage**: Federated entries carry source freshness and caveats.
- **Hubs Route, They Do Not Authorize**: The Attestation Exchange Hub controls validation, filtering, and bounded exchange, preserving any local caveats.
- **Treaty Baselines**: Standardized catalogs mapped across registries for benchmarking.
- **Sovereign Policy Attestation Ecosystems**: A macro-level mapping of participants, capabilities, and visibility, strictly respecting sovereign overrides.
