# Operators Guide: Narrative Federations, Meshes, Clearing Councils, and Atlases

Operators must navigate the evidence atlas without attempting to force unbounded assurance.

## Key Operations
1. **Running an Atlas Pass**: Use `run-evidence-atlas-pass` to update federations, clear cases, and refresh atlas nodes.
2. **Monitoring Mesh Pressure**: Use `preview-assurance-meshes` to detect backpressured or degraded edges. High pressure indicates unresolved staleness or debt.
3. **Reviewing Clearing Councils**: Use `preview-clearing-councils` to monitor open conflicts that require quorum or evidence.

## Important Rule
Stale dashboards or unverified evidence will systematically degrade federated narratives to `stale` or `review_only`.
