# Disclosure and Intake Guide

As an operator, your responsibility is to ensure that the gateway strategies match the current readiness of the system.
- Why publication is redaction-governed: To ensure secrets and internal paths do not leak while proofs remain visible.
- Why challenge intake is review-only: To prevent malicious external actors from altering the internal state or causing denial of service.

See `list-public-gateway-strategies` for options on strategies.
