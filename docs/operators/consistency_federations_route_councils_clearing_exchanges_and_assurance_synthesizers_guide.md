# Consistency Ledger Federations and Sovereign Governance Assurance Synthesizers Architecture

## Why mature assurance surfaces need route councils and assurance synthesizers
Governance needs to unify without creating an autonomous, overriding central authority. Route councils handle complex pathing disputes, while assurance synthesizers cap confidence based on evidence and freshness.

## Consistency Ledger Federation Model
Allows bounded federation without losing contradiction lineage or overriding local sovereignty limits.

## Tribunal Route Council Model
Adjudicates path disputes. If evidence is lacking, councils downgrade paths to review_only rather than denying or blindly accepting.

## Evidence Clearing Exchange Model
Exchanges evidence across boundaries while strictly preserving caveats and scope.

## Sovereign Governance Assurance Synthesizer Model
A synthesizer runs non-authoritative passes to produce an `AssuranceBand`. It caps confidence heavily based on staleness and absence of evidence.
