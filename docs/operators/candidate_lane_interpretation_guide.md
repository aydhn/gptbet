# Candidate Safety Lanes

Operators should interpret the tournament lanes as follows:

1. **Safe Shortlist Lane**: Ready for standard review. Risk is acceptable, and support is strong.
2. **Advisory Lane**: Usually higher risk or lower support. Provides insight but may require manual patching if actionable.
3. **Exploratory Lane**: Lacks sufficient data. Used for learning; do not approve for release.
4. **Blocked Lane**: Violates hard safety constraints (e.g., excessive blast radius).
