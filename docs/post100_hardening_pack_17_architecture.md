# Post-100 Hardening Pack 17 Architecture

This document describes the architectural enhancements introduced in Phase 100 Hardening Pack 17.

## Purpose
The purpose of this hardening pack is to ensure that the planetary continuity verification systems (Observatory Federations, Scheduler Proof Lanes, Audit Pulse Councils, and Continuity Evidence Exchanges) operate honestly, without hiding risks.

## Components
1.  **Observatory Federations:** Ensure bounded observability without overriding local truth.
2.  **Scheduler Proof Lanes:** Carry scheduler truth honestly, tracking freshness, lags, and lineages.
3.  **Audit Pulse Councils:** Act as disciplined evaluators (not authorities) for resolving audit gaps.
4.  **Continuity Evidence Exchanges:** Facilitate bounded exchanges of continuity evidence without expanding scope or losing caveats.

## Key Principles
-   **No Hidden State:** Staleness, lags, residues, and broken lineages must be explicit and visible.
-   **No Authority Overreach:** Federations, councils, and exchanges do not create absolute authority; they evaluate and summarize evidence.
-   **Fail Closed:** If there is doubt (stale evidence, missing quorum, broken lineage), the system defaults to a degraded or blocked state.
-   **Visibility Preservation:** Caveats, 'no-safe' markers, and sovereignty notes must be preserved across boundaries.

## Future Path
This architecture paves the way for continuity arbitration rails, scheduler recovery fabrics, archive proof meshes, and worldwide visibility ledgers.
