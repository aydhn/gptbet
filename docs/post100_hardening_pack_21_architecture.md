# Post-100 Hardening Pack 21: Terminal Lifecycle Hardening

This pack is the terminal convergence phase for the entire ecosystem. Its purpose is not to add features but to establish bounded, explainable, replayable, and audit-friendly lifecycle closure disciplines.

## Objectives
- Ensure terminal closure honesty.
- Produce explicit deprecation maps, avoiding silent removals.
- Transition into maintenance mode while preserving bounded correctness.
- Hand off long-horizon stewardship responsibilities clearly.

## Architecture
- **Closure Bundles:** Preserve truth including caveats, no-safe, and sovereignty indicators.
- **Deprecation Maps:** Clarify supported, frozen, and replaced states.
- **Maintenance Modes:** Establish boundaries without adding features.
- **Long-Horizon Stewardship Packs:** Provide owner visibility, escalation, and cadence.

## Guardrails
- **Stale artifacts** cannot be presented as current truth.
- **Ownership gaps** are treated as blockers.
- **No-safe and sovereignty continuity** must be preserved across maintenance and stewardship handoffs.
