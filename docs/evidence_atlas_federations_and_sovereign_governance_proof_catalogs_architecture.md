# Evidence Atlas Federations & Sovereign Governance Proof Catalogs Architecture

## Purpose
Phase 93 builds on the existing evidence atlas and narrative federation structures. It introduces:
1. Evidence Atlas Federations
2. Narrative Audit Boards
3. Assurance Mesh Observatories
4. Sovereign Governance Proof Catalogs

These structures elevate the system from isolated evidence to connected, federated, and observable assurance meshes.

## Models
### Evidence Atlas Federation
Links multiple atlases. Does not create overriding authority. Preserves currentness and caveat properties from its members.

### Narrative Audit Board
Governs narrative outputs via an auditable review process. Does not act as an execution controller.

### Assurance Mesh Observatory
Monitors assurance meshes for anomalies, degradation, and staleness. Generates alerts and captures snapshots. Non-authoritative.

### Sovereign Governance Proof Catalog
Catalogs governance proofs with lineage, currentness, and applicability details. Proofs are indexable and queryable, providing evidence for audit boards.

## Key Rules
- **No Overriding Local Deny**: Federated evidence cannot override local sovereignty blocks.
- **Strict Currentness**: Stale proofs or federations downgrade confidence and are explicitly labeled.
- **No-Safe Visibility**: Caveats, degraded signals, and no-safe markers must persist through all federation, audit, and catalog queries.
