# Governance Integrity Architecture (Phase 54)

## Overview
This layer provides machine-checkable governance rules ensuring that policy bundles, applied policy snapshots, and governance decisions are cryptographically traceable and tamper-evident.

## Key Concepts
- **Signed Policy Bundles:** Policies packaged with signatures.
- **Immutable Applied Policy Snapshots:** Snapshots linking decisions to the exact policy active at the time.
- **Tamper-Evident Ledger:** An append-only hash chain recording governance events.
- **Decision Proofs:** Cryptographic proofs linking evidence to decisions.
