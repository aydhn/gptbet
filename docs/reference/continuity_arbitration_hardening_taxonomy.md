# Reference: Continuity Arbitration Hardening Taxonomy

- **Arbitration Rail:** A bounded truth evaluation path that respects local precedence and preserves no-safe semantics.
- **Recovery Fabric:** The stitched sequence of pathways and lanes facilitating scheduler recovery, explicitly indicating burden.
- **Proof Mesh:** The verified sequence of cryptographic or structural proofs representing continuous lineage.
- **Visibility Ledger:** The immutable log of visibility state changes, lag spikes, and continuity exceptions.
