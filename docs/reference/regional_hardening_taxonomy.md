# Regional Hardening Taxonomy

- **Failover**: A transition of an active lane from a primary to a secondary.
- **Residue**: The operational artifacts, warnings, or mismatched states left over after a transition wave.
- **Lineage**: The unbroken chain of hash evidence through an archive relocation.
- **No-Safe Visibility**: The preservation of non-safe or restricted caveats, even when regional systems degrade.
