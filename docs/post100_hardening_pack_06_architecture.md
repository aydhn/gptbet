# Post-100 Hardening Pack 06 Architecture

This pack focuses on human-operator readiness, explicit escalation ladders, disaster recovery rehearsals, and governance continuity. It ensures that the operational surface is explainable, audit-friendly, and maintains strict guarantees (caveat preservation, no-safe visibility, sovereignty precedence) even during operator intervention and disaster recovery scenarios.

## Key Concepts
- **Operator Readiness Drills**: Ensures human operators understand the system and can handle signals without masking details.
- **Escalation Ladders**: Explicit routing for when local resolution is insufficient.
- **Disaster Recovery Rehearsals**: Practical drills for restoring state and handling degraded output without overclaiming success.
- **Governance Continuity Audits**: Verification that all actions leave an honest trail and respect core governance boundaries.
