# Health Compiler Federations and Resilience Score Synthesis Architecture

## Objective
This architecture layers health compiler federations, replay workload exchanges, convergence debt ledgers, and sovereign governance resilience score synthesis on top of existing governance stabilization layers.

## Why Synthesis?
Governance health produces isolated outputs. Federation allows aggregation. Replay workloads allow capability verification across boundaries. Convergence debt ledgers track the aging of non-convergence. Synthesis bounds and caps all these to provide an explainable score.

## Key Components
- **Health Compiler Federation**: Aggregates guidance across nodes. Never supersedes local authority.
- **Replay Workload Exchange**: Shares workloads while preserving caveats and evidence boundaries.
- **Convergence Debt Ledger**: Ages and settles debt explicitly. Stale debt caps scores.
- **Resilience Score Synthesis**: Produces bounded bands (e.g., `strong_bounded_resilience`, `review_only_resilience`) driven by federation freshness, debt severity, and local sovereignty.
