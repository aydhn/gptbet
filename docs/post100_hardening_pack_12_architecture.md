# Post-100 Hardening Pack 12 Architecture

## Why Planetary Continuity Honesty Matters
At planetary scales, hiding the friction of timezone handoffs and global data lag introduces systemic risk. The new Planetary Hardening layer ensures every boundary—time, geography, and team shift—is explicitly captured.

## Core Models

### Planetary Coverage Calendar Model
Requires calendars to represent true continuity coverage. We forbid hiding timezone seams and demand explicit ownership for critical events.

### Intercontinental Recovery Lane Model
Transitions across continents cannot simply be 'done'. They are tracked as a governed lane involving source freshness checks, target integrity validation, and documented residue.

### Global Quorum Federation Model
A quorum federation isn't about deciding truth, it's about evaluating agreement. Strong agreement preserves the caveats of each regional node.

### Follow-The-Sun Audit Pack Model
Makes inter-operator handoffs auditable. Unreplayable handoffs or missing acknowledgements result in downgraded continuity health.
