# Reviewer Guide: Post-100 Terminal Lifecycle

As a reviewer, your job is to enforce honesty at the end of the lifecycle.

- **Reject Stale Baselines**: Do not allow frozen, unmaintained artifacts to pass as current operational truth.
- **Reject Silent Deprecation**: If a surface is no longer supported, it must be on the Deprecation Map.
- **Reject Maintenance Concealment**: Maintenance mode summaries should not hide active risks or technical debt residues.
- **Reject Unowned Stewardship**: All long-horizon artifacts require a Reachable owner in the Stewardship Pack.

Visibility of NO-SAFE indicators and SOVEREIGNTY reservations must be carried forward across all transitions.
