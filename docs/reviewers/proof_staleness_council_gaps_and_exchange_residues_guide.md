# Reviewers Guide: Staleness, Gaps, and Residues

When reviewing continuity verification reports, focus on the following:

## Proof Staleness
Stale proofs are the enemy of continuity. If a proof lane is marked 'review_only', it likely contains stale packets. Investigate the source.

## Council Gaps
Councils that resolve cases without sufficient evidence will result in 'review_only' or 'caveated' statuses. Ensure quorum thresholds are met.

## Exchange Residues
If a Continuity Evidence Exchange completes but leaves behind unresolved residues, those residues must be tracked and eventually addressed. They should never be hidden.
