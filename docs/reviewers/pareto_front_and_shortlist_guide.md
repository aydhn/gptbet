# Pareto Front & Shortlist Review Guide

When reviewing tournament results, it's critical to understand that the "winner" is not necessarily the only valuable candidate.

- **Pareto Front 1**: Contains candidates that are not dominated by any other candidate.
- **Shortlist Tier 1**: Reserved for candidates in Front 1 that are also in the Safe Shortlist lane.
- **Why Dominated**: A candidate is dominated if another candidate is better or equal across all objectives, and strictly better in at least one.
