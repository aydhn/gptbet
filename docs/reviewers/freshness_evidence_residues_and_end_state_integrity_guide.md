# Reviewer Guide: Freshness, Evidence Residues, and End-State Integrity

Reviewers must focus on ensuring that the end-state review accurately reflects the underlying evidence, particularly its freshness and any unresolved residues.

## Key Principles
* Stale evidence heavily caps the agreement quality.
* Missing evidence results in caveated or review-only routes.
* `no_safe_recovery_hint` MUST be preserved throughout the pipeline.
