# Reviewer Guide: Archive Migration & Cross-Region Visibility

## Overview
As a reviewer, your role is to identify where visibility or continuity has degraded across a migration or failover.

## Things to Look For
- **Hidden Residue**: Does a cutover summary mask incomplete steps?
- **Hash Continuity**: Does the archive migration maintain unbroken linkage?
- **No-Safe Losses**: Did a live-fire visibility drill result in a suppressed sovereignty note?
