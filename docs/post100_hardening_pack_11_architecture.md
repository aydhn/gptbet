# Post-100 Hardening Pack 11 Architecture

## Why Post-Feature Global Hardening Matters
This module shifts from functionality to planetary constraints: multi-region mesh connectivity, global coverage, operational continuity drills, and cross-region recovery governance. The resilience layer refuses to trust stale states and requires bounded, honest visibility across wide-area networks.

## Core Models
- **Regional Quorum Mesh Model:** Exposes node latency, stale regions, and vote integrity across different time zones.
- **Planetary Coverage Synthesis Model:** Formalizes global operational coverage seamlessly integrating overlaps, seams, handoffs, and gap risks.
- **Global Continuity Drill Model:** Defines scenarios, phased checks, decision paths, gaps, and explicit residue captures for continuous verification.
- **Cross-Region Recovery Governance Model:** Establishes unambiguous approval chains, escalation roles, fallback behaviors, and sovereignty awareness over global boundaries.
- **Worldwide Visibility:** Bounded budgets govern acceptable latency, continuity decay, and acknowledgment lags before halting releases.
