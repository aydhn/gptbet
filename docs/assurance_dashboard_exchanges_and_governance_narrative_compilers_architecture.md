# Assurance Dashboard Exchanges & Governance Narrative Compilers Architecture

## Phase 91 Overview
The Phase 91 layer builds upon resilience synthesis councils, replay exchange marketplaces, and debt settlement planners to introduce:
- Assurance dashboard exchanges
- Council federation boards
- Replay market clearing layers
- Sovereign governance narrative compilers

## Key Principles
1. **Dashboard Exchanges Share Assurance, Not Authority:** Dashboards communicate visibility into metrics, not actionable authority.
2. **Boards Coordinate Deliberation, Not Execution:** Federation boards manage the flow of interpretation and caveats without runtime override.
3. **Clearing Must Stay Bounded:** Replay market clearing adheres to strict evidence constraints and limits scope matching.
4. **Narratives Must Show the System Honestly:** No smoothing over no-safe conditions or caveats.

## Data Flow
- Exchanged packets carry snapshots and required freshness.
- Planners use clearing decisions to affect restoration ceilings contextually.
- Narrative compilers produce strictly scoped and caveated outputs matching audience profiles.
