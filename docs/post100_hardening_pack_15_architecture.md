# Post-100 Hardening Pack 15 Architecture

## Overview
This pack extends the Sovereign Governance Architecture by introducing the Planetary Federation Hardening Layer. The focus is not on new features, but rather ensuring that planetary mesh federations, corridor superchains, scheduler buses, and global audit cadence orchestrations function deterministically while strictly preserving "no-safe" visibility, local sovereignty, and lineage.

## Core Concepts
- **Planetary Mesh Federations**: Agreements between globally distributed meshes that produce bounded, explainable outputs without functioning as an authoritative surface.
- **Corridor Superchains**: Replayable and rollback-ready segment chains that maintain end-to-end lineage continuity across intercontinental archives and handoffs.
- **Scheduler Buses**: Reliable event transport surfaces that ensure global visibility of windows, owners, cadence, and explicit lag without hiding drift.
- **Global Audit Cadence Orchestration**: A strictly measured, honest representation of worldwide audit transitions, seams, and handoffs.

## Design Principles
1. **Federations Must Preserve Local Truth**: Local caveats and no-safe notes are non-negotiable and must propagate.
2. **Superchains Must Preserve Lineage End-to-End**: A break in replayability invalidates the entire chain.
3. **Scheduler Buses Must Transport Scheduling Truth**: Acks and drift must be explicitly mapped.
4. **Audit Cadence Must Be Orchestrated Honestly**: Seams, gaps, and degraded lanes must be visible.
5. **Fail Closed**: Missing segments or unsupported nodes result in blocked or caveated statuses.
