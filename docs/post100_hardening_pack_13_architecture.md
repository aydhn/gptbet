# Post-100 Hardening Pack 13 Architecture

## Planetary Transport Hardening

This pack focuses on verifying the global continuity transport layer. It ensures that context, provenance, caveats, and visibility notes (like `no_safe` and `sovereignty`) are correctly ferried across planetary coverage buses, handoff archives, quorum corridors, and audit calendar simulations.

## Key Layers

1. **Planetary Coverage Buses:** Ensure that freshness, notes, and lag are correctly handled.
2. **Intercontinental Handoff Archives:** Provide a replayable lineage of owner transitions without treating stale data as current.
3. **Quorum Federation Corridors:** Establish boundaries on agreement without granting authority, tracking caveated carry-forwards.
4. **Worldwide Audit Calendar Simulations:** Expose seams, lag, and ownership gaps explicitly across timezones without smoothing.

## Rules

- No stale bus payloads accepted as current.
- Handoff archives must maintain lineage.
- Corridors must carry caveats forward.
- Simulation seams must remain explicit and visible.
