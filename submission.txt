Phase 77: Corridor Governance and Interoperability Scorecards Implementation

1. Phase 77 implementation summary
This phase establishes the Sovereign Interoperability Governance layer. It introduces Discoverable Corridor Catalogs, Continuity Attestations, Treaty Lifecycle Controllers, and Sovereign Remediation Interoperability Scorecards.
The design principles enforce that catalogs are for discovery only (not execution/admission), attestations follow verified continuity, treaties have strict lifecycle state transitions, and scorecards provide explainable benchmarking.
